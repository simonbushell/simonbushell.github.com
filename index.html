About Me

I am a macromolecular crystallographer, specialising in the structural characterisation of membrane proteins from both prokaryotic and eukaryotic organisms. Formerly a research fellow at the Biomolecular Sciences Research Complex at the University of St Andrews, I will be shortly taking up a postdoctoral research position at the Structural Genomics Consortium at the University of Oxford.

I am the creator of RubyPrimer: a web application that enables users to easily design hundreds of site-directed mutagenesis experiments with just a few mouse clicks. I am also the lead developer of DNAnode: an ORM wrapper for related nucleic acid sequences written in Ruby.
